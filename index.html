    This meeting is a pre-launch of the French-Italian-German Laboratoire International Associé, named COPDESC, in applied analysis. More precisely, the themes are : Control and Optimization, Nonlinear PDEs, Calculus of variations, Scientific computing/ Numerical analysis.
